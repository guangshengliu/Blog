C Primer Plus (6th Edition)
By Stephen prata, USA

C Primer Plus (6th Edition) explains the basic concepts and programming skills of C language in detail. This book takes the complete program as an example to explain the knowledge points and precautions of C language. At the end of each chapter, a large number of review questions and programming exercises are designed to help readers consolidate their knowledge and improve their practical programming ability. The appendix gives the reference answers and abundant reference materials of each chapter's review questions. C Primer Plus (6th Edition) can be used as a textbook of C language, which is suitable for beginners who need to learn C language systematically, and also for programmers who want to consolidate C language knowledge or further improve programming technology.

Programming Abas (revision 2)
By Jon Bentley

The author of this book, Jon Bentley, a computer scientist in the world, is known as one of the ten masters who have influenced the development of algorithms.
Over the years, when programmers choose their favorite computer books, "Abas programming" has always been at the forefront. Just as the Pearl in the nature comes from the sand to whet the oyster, Jon Bentley, a master of computer science, with his unique insight and creativity, condensed an immortal "Pearl" of programming from the practical problems of whetting programmers, and became a popular column in the history of ACM communication, a world-famous Computer Science magazine. He has become two immortal classic books of computer science, with influence and creativity. It inspires generations of programmers and computer scientists. This book is the first volume. It mainly discusses the essential problem in Computer Science: how to select and implement algorithms correctly and efficiently. In the book, the author selects many typical complex programming and algorithm problems, vividly describes the anecdotes, detours and continuous improvement of the masters in the history of exploring solutions, guides the readers to think creatively like real programmers and software engineers, and thoroughly expounds and summarizes many unique and exquisite designs. Principles, ways to think and solve problems, and practical programming skills. ​

Refactoring to improve the design of existing code
By Martin Fowler

The immortal classic of software development vividly expounds the principles and specific methods of reconstruction. It is a secret collection that ordinary programmers must cultivate when they are advanced to programming masters.Refactoring, in a word, is to improve the code in an orderly way without changing the external behavior. Many years ago, it was the publication of the original edition of this book that finally made refactoring come out of the small circle of programming experts and become an indispensable part of the daily development work of many ordinary programmers. Therefore, this book has become a classic work with the same name as "design mode", which has been translated into many languages, such as China, Germany, Russia, Japan, etc., and has been selling well all over the world. This book has accumulated the valuable experience gained by the experts in the software development community for many years, and has the value not to be worn out by the passage of time. Today, no matter the refactoring itself, the understanding of the industry, or the support of the development tools for refactoring, it can not be compared with the publication of this book, but the meaning and essence contained in the book are still worth chewing and chewing, and often can be read frequently.
